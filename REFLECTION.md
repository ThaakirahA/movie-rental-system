# Reflection – Balancing Stakeholder Needs

While developing the Aura Reels Movie Rental System, several challenges were encountered when balancing the needs of different stakeholders.

Customers require a system that is fast, easy to use, and visually engaging. They want to quickly search for movies and complete rentals without delays. However, system administrators prioritize system security, reliability, and performance. Implementing strong security measures can sometimes affect system speed, which creates a trade-off between usability and security.

Another challenge was ensuring that administrators and staff could easily manage movie information while keeping the system stable. Developers must design the system in a modular way so that updates to the movie catalog do not affect other parts of the system.

To address these challenges, the system requirements were designed to balance usability, performance, security, and maintainability. Clear functional and non-functional requirements help ensure that the system meets the needs of all stakeholders while remaining scalable and maintainable for future improvements.
