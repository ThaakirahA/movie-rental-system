# Reflection – Balancing Stakeholder Needs

While developing the Aura Reels Movie Rental System, several challenges were encountered when balancing the needs of different stakeholders.

Customers require a system that is fast, easy to use, and visually engaging. They expect quick search results and a seamless rental process. However, system administrators prioritize system security, reliability, and uptime. Implementing strong security measures can sometimes affect system performance, creating a trade-off between usability and security.

Another challenge was ensuring that administrators and staff could efficiently manage movie data without affecting system stability. Developers must design the system using a modular architecture to ensure that updates do not disrupt other system components.

Additionally, business owners focus on user engagement and growth, while developers focus on technical quality and maintainability. These priorities must be balanced to ensure both business success and system sustainability.

To address these challenges, the system requirements were carefully defined to balance usability, performance, security, and maintainability. Clear and measurable functional and non-functional requirements ensure that the system meets stakeholder needs while remaining scalable and adaptable for future improvements.
